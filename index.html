ACOUSTIC VIBE STUDIOS

The Situation

Acoustic Vibe Studios is located in Athens and it consists of 2 separate -Acoustically Treated- rooms (9 sq.m. and 4 sq.m. respectively). The larger one acts both as a recording but also as a mixing room, where all the equipment is settled, while the smaller one is especially designed for Instrument and Vocal Recordings. The wooden floor, bass traps, foam absorbers, diffusers along with the extra wooden parts placed on the walls, all constitute and even reinforce a very natural sound atmosphere inside both rooms.


Applications

The studio can be used either for individual recordings, thus each musician is recorded separately, or it can host up to 3-4 musician performing and being recorded concurrently. If electric instruments are to be used, then depending on the setup an extra space can be used, hosting up to 6-7 people in total.
